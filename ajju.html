Highlights

Here are some key facts about Usain Bolt:

  1. Usain St. Leo Bolt is often called "Lightning Bolt."
  2. He is a Jamaican sprinter widely considered the greatest sprinter of all time.
  3. He is an eight-time Olympic gold medalist.
  4. He holds the world record for the 100-meter sprint.
  5. He holds the world record for the 200-meter sprint.
  6. He also holds the world record in the 4x100-meter relay.
